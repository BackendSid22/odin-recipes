PB Smoothie

Description

This banana oat smoothie is smooth and creamy, the perfect consistency, just like a smoothie should be!

You will taste hints of honey, vanilla, peanut butter, and banana.

The smoothie is very filling and great for an on-the-go breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, broken in half
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1/4 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 teaspon honey, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste

Steps

  1. Put banana, ice, oats, yogurt, skim milk, peanut butter, honey, and cinnamon, respectively, in a blender;
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Garnish with Chia seeds
